Welcome stranger

QuizBubbles is intended for fun and educational purposes, making it easy for everyone to add a bundle of questions and challenge each other in two quiz modes.

Add questions

Once you created your bubble, it's time to add questions. Take your time and enjoy the creativity. Use the settings to change name, password or if your bubble is publicly accessible.

Sprint

Sprint selects 12 questions in ascending difficulty and a single wrong answer means game over.

Create your bubble

A bubble is your little universe holding questions. Creating one takes seconds, no registration required.

Time to play

There are two quiz modes to challenge your friends: Sprint and Marathon. Your performance is ranked based on the number of correct answers and the time.

Marathon

Marathon is all about endurance, providing you with all questions available and counting how many you get right.
