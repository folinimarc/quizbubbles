Welcome stranger

QuizBubbles makes it easy and fun to test your knowledge and contribute questions. Ideal as classroom exercise, for company newcomers or having fun with friends.

Add questions

Once you created your bubble, it's time for you and your friends to add questions. Take your time and enjoy the creativity.

Sprint

Sprint selects 12 questions in ascending difficulty and a single wrong answer means game over.

Public bubbles

Public bubbles allow people to play a quiz without password by just sharing the link. Adding questions still requires a password. Change public accessibility in bubble settings.

Create your bubble

A bubble is your little universe holding questions. Creating one takes seconds, no registration required.

Time to play

There are two quiz modes: Sprint and Marathon. Your performance is ranked based on the number of correct answers and the time.

Marathon

Marathon is all about endurance, providing you with all questions available and counting how many you get right.

That's all!
